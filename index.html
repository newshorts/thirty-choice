Help Select 10 for 30

Believe it or not, Goodby Sliverstein & Partners will celebrate its 30th anniversary on April 15.

After the party for our 25th, we decided that our collective livers can only handle a celebration like that once every ten years.

Our 30th is going to be a more low-key affair. But one way we’re commemorating the milestone is to compile a ranking of the “top 10” things we’ve made in our first 30 years.

We’re reaching out to a small, select list of distinguished GSP alumni (like yourself) to rank the pieces. This list will be decided by your votes, not ours. If you have a few minutes, we’d love to hear from you.

Some of the agency’s greatest hits are assembled on this site. You can rank them or write-in another campaign you remember from your years here.

Thanks for joining us on the ride.

 - Jeff & Rich

Votes are due by 5:00 p.m. PDT, Monday, March 25.
